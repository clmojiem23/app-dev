**My favorite movie is Interstellar**

Interstellar is a science fiction movie directed by Christopher Nolan. It tells the story of a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity.

**Plot Summary**

“Mankind was born on Earth. It was never meant to die here.”

The film follows Cooper, a former NASA pilot, and a team of explorers as they journey across space to find a habitable planet. On Earth, his daughter Murph works to solve the equation for gravity to save humanity.

**Main Characters**

Cooper – The brave pilot and father    
Murph – His intelligent and determined daughter    
Dr. Brand – The scientist leading the mission    

**Why I Like It**

 Amazing visuals    
 Emotional and thought-provoking story    
 Beautiful music by Hans Zimmer    

**Favorite Scene**
The scene where Cooper finally reunites with Murph after decades apart always makes me cry.

**Fun Fact**
That black hole — Gargantua — was rendered so accurately that scientists published a paper about its physics!
